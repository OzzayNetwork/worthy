About Us

Worthy Credit Capitals is a credit only Micro-Finance Company licensed in Kenya under the Company’s Act. The company, was founded with a vision to provide emergency financial solutions across the country and beyond. By leveraging on technology and a world class customer experience, we continue to transform lives by delivering CASH IN 24 HOURS.

We have continued to grow to become a major regional player with a footprint in all counties in Kenya, having served over 1000 clients countrywide. Our wide range of Loan products are tailor made to meet your various financial needs and foster lasting relationships.
