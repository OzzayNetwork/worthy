Import Duty Financing requirements

    • Bill of lading
    • Jevic / Export Certificate

Logbook Loans requirements

  • Original motor vehicle logbook
  • KRA PIN of the applicant
  • Original national ID card of the applicant
  • Two passport size photos
  • 12 months bank statement
  • Comprehensive insurance certificate cover

Invoice Discount Loans requirements

  • Copy of the original invoice duly acknowledged by the procuring entity.
  • Delivery notes for the goods supplied.
  • Acceptable collateral
  • Latest bank statements for the business.
  • ID copy of the applicant.

LPO Financing Loans requirements

  • KRA and ID copy of the applicant
  • Passport photos
  • Original LPO/ LSO/ Contract.
  • Pro-forma invoices for the goods to be supplied/ break-down of costs.
  • Tangible security eg. Logbook or Title Deed.
  • Evidence of similar past jobs done through past invoices and delivery notes.

Title Deed Loans requirements

  • KRA and ID copy of the applicant
  • 12 Months bank statement
  • Copy of the Title Deed
  • Valuation report of property
  • Legal charge over the property
